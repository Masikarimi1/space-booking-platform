AI Travel Assistant Tips

Preparing for Low Gravity

For your upcoming Lunar trip, consider doing resistance training before departure. This helps your body adjust to the 1/6th gravity on the Moon and prevents muscle atrophy during your stay.

Photography Tips

The stark contrast between light and shadow on the lunar surface requires special camera settings. Pack a polarizing filter and adjust your exposure settings to capture the true beauty of the Moon.

Packing Essentials

Remember that all essential space gear will be provided, but bring personal items like lip balm and lotion as the regulated environment can cause dryness. Personal mementos (under 100g) are also permitted.
